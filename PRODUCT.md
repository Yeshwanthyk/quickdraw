# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Agents and senior engineers who need to create, annotate, inspect, and pass around PNG artifacts quickly from a local command line. Human users enter the browser canvas only when refinement is faster by hand.

## Product Purpose

quick-paint turns screenshots, clipboard images, and structured scene specs into durable PNG artifacts under `/tmp`, then copies or prints those artifacts back into the agent workflow.

## Brand Personality

Fast, precise, utilitarian.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-site ornament, decorative canvas chrome, modal-heavy flows, and generic AI app gradients. The interface should feel closer to a focused native utility than a presentation tool.

## Design Principles

- AI-first path before human refinement.
- Keep the drawing surface dominant and the controls compact.
- Prefer deterministic file artifacts over hidden app state.
- Make visual feedback immediate during drag, save, and selection.
- Preserve simple command-line contracts for agents.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keyboard shortcuts must have button equivalents. Interactive controls should keep at least 40px effective hit targets where practical, visible focus states, readable contrast, and reduced-motion-friendly transitions.
